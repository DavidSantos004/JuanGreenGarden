Welcome to JuanGreenGarden

JuanGreenGarden is a project aimed at supporting the operations of a gardening company. It covers various areas, from office details and employees to the management of customers, orders, products, and payments. The project utilizes Java programming language, Spring Boot framework, PostgreSQL database for development. Our goal is to create a web application with both backend and frontend, adhering to best development practices.

We are dedicated to providing an intuitive and engaging user experience by leveraging modern web technologies. Our team continuously improves and expands the platform to meet the needs of our growing community.
