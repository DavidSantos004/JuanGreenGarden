Spring Boot Project - Gardening

  • Summary:

  • This project aims to develop software that supports the operations of a gardening sector company. Its structure encompasses various areas, from office and employee details to customer, order, product, and payment management. Java programming language, Spring Boot framework, and MySQL or PostgreSQL database will be used for development. The scope of this development is limited to creating a web application with backend and frontend with good development practices.

  • Difficulty Level: 5/5

  • Duration: 7 days
Specifications

  • For this project, there is a database, named "gardening", designed to support the operations and management of a gardening company. In its structure, various tables have been defined covering key aspects of business operations, from internal office organization and employee management to detailed tracking of products, orders, and financial transactions with customers.

  • The "office" table stores information about the company's different locations, including details such as city, country, and postal code. On the other hand, the "employee" table covers data related to personnel, such as names, positions, and hierarchical relationships. Product management is carried out through the "product" table, which includes details such as name, range, and stock quantity.

  • Interaction with customers is recorded in the "customer" table, where contact information, credit limits, and sales representatives are stored. Orders placed by customers are detailed in the "order" table, while the "order_detail" table provides specific information about the products included in each order, such as quantity and price.

  • Additionally, the database addresses financial aspects through the "payment" table, which records transactions made by customers, detailing payment method, date, and total amount. Product range management is defined in the "product_range" table, which contains descriptions and links to images for each category of products offered.

  • Together, this database (based on MySQL) provides an organized structure to support the comprehensive operation of a gardening company, covering everything from internal management to interactions with customers and associated financial transactions.
